Witbank Skydiving Club has been in continuous existence since 1978. We are a non-profit club where the emphasis is on fun skydives and a great social scene. We operate on weekends and public holidays - weather permiting. The club owns it's own Cessna 206 Turbo and an upgraded Cessna 205 aircraft, student equipment and buildings.

The club members are involved in most skydiving disciplines: we have active

Formation Skydivers (Relative Work)
Freeflyers
Tandem Masters
Skyboarders
Video Camermen

When we're not skydiving we enjoy the odd party (and some of them have got VERY odd).

Most people make a weekend of it and spend saturday night at the club where you can either grab a matress and sleep in the main club house or pitch a tent. See the seperate facilities page for more information on what ground facilities we have available.

The club also runs an annual long weekend getaway/mini-boogie to Barberton in the Eastern Transvaal, this gives us all a chance to enjoy some of the beautiful scenery in that area as well as introducing some of the locals to skydiving.
