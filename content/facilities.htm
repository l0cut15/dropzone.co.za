Ground Facilities

Because of the distance from Johannesburg and Pretoria (about 120Km from both, click here for directions) most people like to make a weekend out of their trip to Witbank and stay the Saturday night at the club (the real hard core arrive on Friday afternoon so they can get in two nights of partying and lots of skydives). The club has a pile of matresses, which anyone can use for sleeping in the clubhouse overnight, these are available on a strictly first come first serve basis. Alternatively you may bring your own tent and pitch it on the drop zone. Hot showers and toilet facilities are available in the main clubhouse.

We have a large braai area which gets used every weekend and braai packs may be purchased from our lovely food/bar lady, Pauline (get in early though). Pauline also supplies hamburgers, cooldrinks and chocolates during the day and once the jumping is finished she runs the always popular bar, supplying beer and spirits. For those not in to roughing it Witbank is a large town with motels and restaurants available (you don't know what you will be missing though).
